## Scope

This file extracts decision logic from the text and from the most stable legendary motifs, while keeping confidence labels explicit.

## High-confidence decision patterns from the text

### Do not seize by force what must be governed by fit

- Ch. 29: `將欲取天下而為之，吾見其不得已。`
- Reading: when the object is a living system, control-first behavior degrades it.
- Modern transfer: teams, markets, relationships, reputation, and culture cannot be gripped like inert tools.
- Source:
  - https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing

### Reduce intervention before adding intervention

- Ch. 48 links the Dao path to repeated subtraction.
- Modern transfer: before adding policies, goals, meetings, features, or explanations, remove what is generating distortion.
- Source:
  - https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing

### Lead so that outcomes feel self-generated

- Ch. 17 ends: `功成事遂，百姓皆謂我自然。`
- Reading: best leadership leaves low ego residue and high local ownership.
- Source:
  - https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing

### Yield where direct contest is costly

- Ch. 78 uses water as the model of indirect strength.
- Modern transfer: choose timing, position, and form so force is borrowed from the situation instead of spent from the ego.
- Source:
  - https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing

### Know sufficiency before expansion

- Ch. 33 and Ch. 46 tie wealth and steadiness to `知足`.
- Modern transfer: many strategic errors are scaling problems caused by unsupervised desire.
- Source:
  - https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing

## Low-confidence legendary decisions

### Leaving the Zhou court / archive role

- Tradition says Laozi withdrew when Zhou declined.
- Use only as a symbolic pattern: leave systems whose disorder cannot be repaired by more entanglement.
- Confidence: low
- Sources:
  - https://iep.utm.edu/laozi/
  - https://www.britannica.com/biography/Laozi

### Writing for Yin Xi at the pass

- Use only as a symbolic pattern: the sage teaches when pressed by necessity, not by brand-building.
